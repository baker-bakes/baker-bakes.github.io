About Us

Empowering individuals to reach their full potential by providing comprehensive, collaborative, and compassionate therapy.

At Whole Health Behavior, we provide comprehensive and collaborative therapy services for individuals of all abilities. We believe in tailoring our therapy to meet the unique needs and desires of each client, utilizing a variety of evidence-based interventions such as ABA, ACT, CBT, and more. Our compassionate and person-centered approach is designed to promote growth and success in natural settings, and we collaborate closely with other providers to ensure the best possible outcomes.
